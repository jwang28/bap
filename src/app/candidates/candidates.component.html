CANDIDATE REQUIREMENTS
In order to apply for membership, candidates must have at least a 3.50 overall GPA, be of freshman standing (> 16 credits) and also plan to major in Accounting, Finance and/or Information Systems. Candidates must complete an application, which can be found under the Candidates Application Links section. A resume, headshot, signed agreement form must be submitted with ALL applications.

To be initiated as a member, candidates must complete all requirements as outlined on the Candidate Agreement Form and in the Beta Alpha Psi Bulletin. Below is a list of the Professional, Social and Community Service points that must be fulfilled by all candidates in order to cross:
Professional Events
Points Required: 16
Each Professional Speaker Event is worth 1 point, unless stated otherwise. Workshops are also worth 1 point. Each Corporate Mingle is worth 2 points.
Social Events
Points Required: 9
Each Social Event is worth 1 point. Going to tutoring hours is 1/2 point - there is a cap of 1 point.
Community Service
Hours Required: 8
Community Service hours will be awarded according to real hours at each Community Service Event.
